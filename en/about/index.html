Where is this?

Welcome to DailyMinz, my personal blog. You can find all kinds of knowledge, feelings, observations and insights here. If you want, you can even contribute to the improvement of this blog on GitHub!

This blog is not limited to the content of a specific field, but is dedicated to recording the bits and pieces of my learning and thinking, in order to leave a trace for my spiritual journey and provide a little insight into this still chaotic world.

This blog attempts to innovate on the traditional form. You can participate in the construction of this blog at any time through the GitHub community, provide your thoughts and insights, and inspire those who come after you.
